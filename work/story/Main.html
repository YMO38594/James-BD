The Sole King Trials
ACT I: TWILIGHT DEPARTURE
[ASGARD - GOLDEN PALACE]
The All-Father walks calmly through the golden palace of Asgard. Every servant he passes receives a smile, a gesture of peace in the midst of what he knows will be storm.
His steps are light, but his gaze is sharp with focus. The conversation with the King of Hell, locked still echoes in his mind. Without unity, the demons of the heart will continue to grow stronger.
With this truth in hand, he sets out to inform his wife, Harribel, Queen of Hueco Mundo and co-ruler of Asgard.
She senses the weight in his words before he speaks them. In understanding of his burden, the two leave one another with a promise to return.
He ponders whether to tell Starrk or Nelliel of his plan, but chooses to inform only one, at random, to avoid the trouble of getting chewed out by them both.
The All-Father leaves for Soul Society.
[SOUL SOCIETY - 1ST DIVISION GROUNDS]
Escorted by Captains Shunsui Kyoraku and Soi Fon, he is led to meet with Captain Commander Genryusai Shigekuni Yamamoto. There, over a cup of lavender tea, they share a final conversation..
ACT II: GINNUNGAGAP AND THE HOURGLASS
[THE FAR REACHES OF GARGANTA]
The All-Father travels to the farthest edges of the Garganta, to where light and shadow cease. There, he creates Ginnungagap, a primordial void from which no reality leaks.
[GINNUNGAGAP - PRIMORDIAL SHRINE]
Within the Ginnungagap, he constructs a shrine. The Three Norns—daughters of his deceased friend Kazuma, the former bearer of Mimir—appear to assist him.
Together, they pierce the Hourglass of Time.
Every moment—past, present, and future—becomes visible. Temporal reishi leaks from the fractures, which is essential to power the ritual known as the Sole King Trials.
For three days and three nights, the All-Father and the Norns gather temporal reishi. The worlds of Soul Society, Hueco Mundo, Asgard, and the Living World tremble in response. The Reimyaku—the pulse of each world—flickers erratically.

ACT III: THE SUPERWEAPON'S SHADOW
[WANDENREICH – THRONE ROOM]
The air is thick with the scent of burning incense and blood. Yhwach stands before his assembled Sternritter, his shadow stretching unnaturally long across the shattered marble floor. The ruins of the Wandenreich seem to breathe around him, walls pulsing like living flesh.
It had been a year. One year since they were forced to flee from the main base in Germany when that bastard soul-reaper and his arrancar friend had reduced this city to a spiritual Dead-Zone. And crippled some of their finest.
But now was the time for reckoning.
YHWACH
"The Soul Reapers and the Arrancar cling to their precious Balance... like drowning men clutching driftwood."
His eyes sweep over his army—Bazz-B's smoldering impatience, As Nodt's silent trembling, Haschwalth's unwavering gaze, even whilst confined to a wheelchair.
Then, his eyes turn to Uryu, who is standing by his side.
YHWACH
"But the tide does not ask for permission before it rises."
Behind him, a monstrous machine hums to life. Its surface is smooth, obsidian-black, etched with glowing Quincy crosses that pulse like a heartbeat. The air distorts around it, reality itself seeming to fray at the edges.
YHWACH
"Today, they shall all witness the punishment of God."
The machine comes to life at those words. And the world turns blue.
